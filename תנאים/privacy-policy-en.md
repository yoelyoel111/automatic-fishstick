# Privacy Policy - Hagralomat

**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Introduction

Hagralomat ("the Site", "the Service", "we") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using the Hagralomat website.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Image Files**: Images and stickers you upload to the site
- **Excel/CSV Files**: Files containing names for lottery purposes
- **Text**: Words and text you add to stickers
- **Projects**: Project data you save

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: Google Analytics for service improvement
- **Technical Information**: Browser type, operating system, IP address
- **Cookies**: For saving language preferences and settings

### Google Integration
- **Google Drive**: Optional connection to save projects
- **Google Account Info**: Name and profile picture (with consent only)

## How We Use Information

### Local Processing
- Most processing occurs in your browser
- Image files and data are processed locally
- No automatic upload to external servers

### Cloud Storage (Optional)
- Save projects to Google Drive only with your consent
- Access to your files only with explicit permission

### Service Improvement
- Analyze usage data to improve functionality
- Identify and fix technical issues

## Information Sharing

**We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties**, except:

- **Google Drive**: Only if you choose to connect and save projects
- **Google Analytics**: Anonymous data for usage analysis
- **Legal Requirements**: In exceptional cases of legal demand

## Data Security

### Technical Protections
- HTTPS encryption for all communication
- Local processing in browser
- No permanent storage on servers

### Physical Protections
- Secure servers with advanced protections
- Encrypted backups
- Limited access to authorized staff only

## Your Rights

### Access and Control
- **View**: See all saved data
- **Edit**: Change or update information
- **Delete**: Remove projects and data
- **Export**: Download your data

### Withdraw Consent
- Disconnect from Google Drive at any time
- Delete cookies from browser
- Stop using the service

## Data Retention

### Retention Periods
- **Local Data**: Stored in browser until manual deletion
- **Google Drive**: According to Google's policy and your choice
- **Analytics Data**: 26 months (Google's policy)

### Automatic Deletion
- Cleanup of temporary files at session end
- Deletion of inactive data after extended period

## Policy Changes

### Change Notifications
- Updates will be posted on the site
- Material changes will include prominent notice
- Continued use constitutes agreement to changes

### Previous Versions
- Previous versions available for review
- Update dates clearly indicated

## Children's Privacy

The service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under this age.
